T'17 Mains : The Quantum Escape (Hauts)
The event introduced students to the world of Game Development! The students were taught the game developing software Construct 2. A lot of thrill and excitement ensued as the students strategized to get across the maze after finding the optimum route. Once they got through the maze, they had to use the utilities of the software to debug a buggy game. This event got the students’ creative juices running, resulting in a variety of solutions.
