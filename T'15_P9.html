T'15 Mains : Prize Distribution
"As a man sows, so shall he reap", it is said. And rightly so, the winners of Technothlon '15 were awarded various interesting prizes, which included Microsoft Kinect for the first, Ollie 1.0 for the second, Raspberry Pi for the third and Arduino for the fourth and fifth positions in the Hauts Squad. The Junior squad winners were awarded an iBall Pad for the first position, Ollie 1.0 for the second, Amazon Kindle for the third and Arduino for the fourth and fifth positions. Consolation prizes were distributed among the rest of the top 50 teams which included a set of Digit Magazines, Technothlon T-shirts and e-learning vouchers. "And those students made their mark permanent by signing on an "I WAS HERE" poster, thus making a mark in the history of Technothlon that'll last forever "
