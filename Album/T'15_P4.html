T'15 Mains : Air Crash Investigation (Hauts)
#Event4 "Air Crash Investigation" - "Once you eliminate the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth..." In this second event of Hauts squad, the students had to use their "detective instincts" to unravel a mystery which led to the crash of a hypothetical aircraft, using the hints and clues provided to them in the form of graphs and photographs picked up by radars and satellites. The young detectives also got a hand-on experience on the basic techniques of Cryptography. Congratulations Tarun and Anubhav, the winners of this event. #T15
