T'17 Mains : An Interactive Session with Dr. Thomas Barclay
Technothlon has been providing students across India a once in a lifetime experience since its inception, and this year was no different. This year, the top 50 teams in both the Juniors and Hauts categories got an incredible opportunity to meet and interact with Dr. Tom Barclay, Research Scientist at NASA and previously the Director of Kepler and K2 Science Guest Observer Offices. The students made the most of this opportunity, and bombarded Dr. Barclay with a variety of questions, which resulted in it being a great learning experience for everyone involved.
